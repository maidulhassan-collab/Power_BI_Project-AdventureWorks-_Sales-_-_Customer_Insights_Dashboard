# Power BI Project - AdventureWorks Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard  
  
![A glimpse of project](https://github.com/maidulhassan-collab/Power_BI_Project-AdventureWorks-_Sales-_-_Customer_Insights_Dashboard/blob/main/AdventureWorks.gif)  

## Project Overview
This project uses the AdventureWorks dataset to build an interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing sales, revenue, profit, product performance, and customer demographics. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance, helping identify trends, top products, and customer behavior patterns.  

## Objectives
- Track overall revenue, profit, and orders with monthly trends.
- Identify top products by orders, revenue, and return rates.
- Analyze product categories (Accessories, Bikes, Clothing) and their demand.
- Explore customer demographics (income level, occupation, region).
- Provide actionable insights for sales strategy and inventory management.

## Key Insights  
### Executive Overview    

![Main Dashboard](https://github.com/maidulhassan-collab/Power_BI_Project-AdventureWorks-_Sales-_-_Customer_Insights_Dashboard/blob/main/main_dashboard.gif)  

The primary dashboard provides a high‑level snapshot of business performance. It combines key financial metrics, monthly trends, and product insights into one view designed for quick decision‑making.  

**Key Metrics**  
- Revenue  
- Profit  
- Orders  
- Return Rate  
These KPIs summarize overall company performance, giving immediate visibility into sales, profitability, and operational efficiency.  

**Monthly Trends**  
- Revenue Trending Chart: Tracks monthly revenue from Jan 2020 to Jan 2022, showing growth patterns and seasonal fluctuations.  
- Month‑over‑Month Changes: This section highlights how performance evolves over time, making it easy to spot improvements or declines.  

**Product Insights**  
- Top 10 Products Table: Lists products by orders, revenue, and return percentage.  
- Category Breakdown: This section identifies best‑selling products and highlights areas with higher return rates, supporting inventory and quality control decisions.  

### Regional Analysis  

![Rgional Analysis](https://github.com/maidulhassan-collab/Power_BI_Project-AdventureWorks-_Sales-_-_Customer_Insights_Dashboard/blob/main/map_dashboard.gif)  
  
This page focuses on geographic performance, showing how sales and orders vary across different regions and countries.

**Regional Breakdown**
- Regions Covered: North America, Europe, Pacific, Asia, Africa, South America, Australia.  
- Each region highlights country‑level performance (e.g., United States, Canada, United Kingdom, France, Germany, Australia).  
- Provides a map visualization to compare sales distribution globally.  

**Insights**
- North America and Europe emerge as major revenue contributors, with strong order volumes.  
- Pacific region shows notable growth in certain product categories.  
- Regional differences highlight market opportunities and areas where return rates or demand patterns vary.  

**Purpose**  
This page helps stakeholders understand where sales are coming from geographically, identify strong markets, and spot regions that may need targeted strategies for growth or customer engagement.  

### Product Analysis  

![Product Analysis](https://github.com/maidulhassan-collab/Power_BI_Project-AdventureWorks-_Sales-_-_Customer_Insights_Dashboard/blob/main/product_dashboard.gif)  

This page drills down into the performance of products and compares theirs results against set business targets.  

**Product KPIs**  
- Monthly Orders vs Target: Shows actual orders compared to target values, highlighting whether the product is meeting demand expectations.  
- Monthly Revenue vs Target: Tracks revenue performance against goals, with clear indicators of under‑ or over‑performance.  
- Monthly Profit vs Target: Displays profitability trends, helping assess whether the product contributes effectively to overall margins.  
- Price Adjustment (%): Allows simulation of price changes and their impact on profit.

**Trend Analysis**  
- Charts illustrate profit and revenue changes over time (Jul 2021 – May 2022).  
- Users can toggle metrics (Orders, Revenue, Profit, Return, Return %) to focus on specific aspects of product performance.  
- Adjusted profit visualization shows how pricing strategies could influence outcomes.  

**Purpose**
This page is designed for product managers and analysts to evaluate individual product performance. It helps answer questions like:  

- Is the product meeting sales and revenue targets?  
- How do returns affect profitability?  
- What would happen if prices were adjusted?

### Customer Insights

![Customer Insights](https://github.com/maidulhassan-collab/Power_BI_Project-AdventureWorks-_Sales-_-_Customer_Insights_Dashboard/blob/main/customer_dashboard.gif)  

This page focuses on customer demographics and behavior, providing a deeper understanding of who the buyers are and how they contribute to overall revenue.  

**Customer Overview**
- Total Customers  
- Revenue Per Customer  
These KPIs highlight the size of the customer base and the value each customer contributes.

**Demographic Analysis**
- Orders by Income Level: High Income, Average Income, Low Income.  
- Orders by Occupation: Professionals, Management, Skilled workers.  
This breakdown shows how different income groups and occupations drive demand, helping identify target customer segments.

**Top Customers**
Top 100 Customers Table: Lists individual customers with their orders and revenue contribution. This section highlights loyal and high‑value customers, useful for retention strategies and personalized marketing.

**Purpose**
This page is designed to provide customer intelligence:  
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Which income and occupation groups contribute most to sales?
- How can marketing and sales strategies be tailored to maximize customer value?
